## KDP Upload Steps

### 1. Go to https://kdp.amazon.com

Sign in (or create account). You'll need:
- Email address
- Tax information (W-8BEN for non-US residents)
- Bank account for royalty payments

### 2. Create New Title → Kindle eBook

### 3. Book Details

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Language** | English |
| **Title** | 2,500 Years of Wellness |
| **Subtitle** | The Yellow Emperor's Guide to Living Well |
| **Author** | Wan Wei |
| **Description** | Copy from `amazon-description.html` |
| **Publishing Rights** | I own the copyright |
| **Keywords (7)** | See below |
| **Categories** | See below |

### 4. Keywords (pick 7)

1. `traditional chinese medicine wellness`
2. `huangdi neijing yellow emperor`
3. `holistic health ancient wisdom`
4. `TCM modern science`
5. `circadian rhythm health`
6. `qigong breathing exercises`
7. `AI health wellness technology`

### 5. Categories (pick 2)

- Health, Fitness & Dieting > Alternative Medicine > Traditional Chinese Medicine
- Health, Fitness & Dieting > Alternative Medicine > Holistic

### 6. Manuscript & Cover Upload

- Upload `2500-Years-of-Wellness.epub`
- Upload `cover.png`
- Enable DRM: **Yes** (recommended)
- Use KDP Previewer to check formatting

### 7. Pricing

| Option | Price Range | Royalty |
|--------|-------------|---------|
| 70% royalty | $2.99 – $9.99 | 70% minus delivery cost |
| 35% royalty | $0.99 – $200 | 35% flat |

**Recommended price: $4.99 – $7.99** (70% royalty tier)

At $6.99:
- 70% royalty = ~$4.89 per sale
- Delivery cost deducted (based on file size, ~$0.10 for this book)

### 8. Publish

- Review everything
- Click "Publish Your Kindle eBook"
- Review takes 24–72 hours
- Book goes live on Amazon once approved

## Post-Publishing

- **Author Central**: Set up your author page at https://author.amazon.com
- **A+ Content**: Add rich media to your book page (requires brand registry)
- **Price Promotions**: Use KDP Select for Kindle Unlimited + promotional tools
- **Reviews**: Reach out to beta readers for honest reviews

## Notes

- The EPUB contains SVG diagrams. KDP will convert them during processing.
  If any images don't render, convert SVGs to PNG using Inkscape or an online tool.
- The cover meets minimum KDP specs (1600×2560). For best results on product
  pages, consider a professionally designed cover later.
